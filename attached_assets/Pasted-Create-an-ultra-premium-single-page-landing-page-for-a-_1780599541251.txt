Create an ultra-premium, single-page landing page for a refrigerator repair master named Alexander in Tver, Russia.

**GLOBAL STYLE & TECH STACK:**
- Framework: React + Vite + TypeScript.
- Styling: Tailwind CSS.
- Animations: Framer Motion for page transitions and scroll reveals. GSAP for complex timeline animations. Three.js (@react-three/fiber + @react-three/drei) for the 3D scene.
- Fonts: 'Space Grotesk' for headings (bold, futuristic), 'JetBrains Mono' for subtext/accents (monospace, tech feel).
- Color Scheme: True Black (#000000) background, White (#FFFFFF) text, Electric Blue (#004FFF) as the single, striking accent color. No other colors.
- Cursor: Custom cursor that is a small dot with a blurry blue glow. On hoverable elements, it scales to 2x and becomes a ring. On the main button, it shows a 'circuit board' pattern.
- Scrollbar: Hide the default scrollbar. Implement a subtle, thin vertical line on the right edge of the screen (height 30px, blue glow) that moves smoothly down to indicate scroll progress.
- Micro-interactions: Every button, link, or interactive card has a smooth hover state (scale, color shift, or border glow). No abrupt changes.

**Section 1: Hero (Full screen, 100vh)**
- Background: Deep black. In the center, a very subtle WebGL shader background effect (slowly moving dark blue and black gradient noise, like smoke underwater).
- Layout: Split screen. Left 40% is the text. Right 60% is the Master's photo.
- Master's Photo: A high-quality portrait (use a professional placeholder of a confident man). The image must have a 'high contrast' black and white filter. Apply a 'parallax' effect where the image moves slightly in the opposite direction of the mouse cursor (bounded within its frame, using Framer Motion's `useMotionValue`). Under the image, a subtle electric blue glowing underline (like a neon sign).
- Headline: "ИНЖЕНЕРНЫЙ РЕМОНТ ХОЛОДИЛЬНИКОВ" (ENGINEERING REFRIGERATOR REPAIR). It types out letter by letter on load (typewriter effect).

- All text must be in Russian (Cyrillic).
- Generate placeholder images (preferably black and white portraits and appliance parts) or use abstract gradients as placeholders. Do NOT use generic stock photos of smiling repairmen.
